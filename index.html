How to Smoke Ribs

Directions

  • Start smoker, set temp to 250F.
  • Season ribs with BBQ Seasoning.
  • Place ribs on smoker, smoke for 3 hours.
  • After three hours, pull ribs and wrap in foil. Continue smoking for two more hours.
  • After two hours, pull ribs and remove foil. Return back to smoker and baste with favorite BBQ sauce. Smoke for an additional hour.
  • After one hour, pull ribs and let rest for at least 20 minutes, uncovered. Then EAT!
